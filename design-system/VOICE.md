# Voice & copy

> **All rules below are DERIVED from the approved homepage copy** — `brand/` (positioning, expression, anti-brand) does not exist yet. When `/plan-brand` runs, re-base this against `brand/07-positioning.md`, `brand/08-expression-on-site.md`, `brand/09-anti-brand.md`. Until then, the homepage snapshot is the source.

## Archetype (derived)
The **honest local tradesman** — plain-spoken, reassuring, no fluff. Confident but not salesy. Sounds like Jamie talking to a neighbour.

## Person
- **First person singular** — Jamie speaks as **"I / me / my"** ("I'm based in Burgess Hill", "I'll get back to you"). The business is "JDH Gas Services".
- Address the reader as **"you / your"**.
- Never corporate "we" (it's a sole trader) and never third-person about Jamie in marketing copy.

## Sentence length & reading level
- Target **≤ 18 words**, ceiling **26**. Break long sentences into two.
- Reading level **~Grade 7–8** (consumer). Clear, jargon-free language; explain terms (e.g. "CP12 — the Landlord Gas Safety Certificate").

## CTA verbs
- **Allowed for primary CTAs:** **Call**, **WhatsApp** (and "WhatsApp me"), **Book**, **Send** (enquiry), **Get a quote**, **Request a quote**.
- **"Learn more"** is permitted **only** as a tertiary card affordance link (e.g. on service cards), never as the main page CTA.
- **Banned:** "Click here", "Submit", "Welcome", "Get started", "Discover".

## Banned words / patterns
- **Em dashes (—)** — banned in body copy (they're the #1 AI tell; we removed 31 of them). Use a full stop, comma, or "and". (The single permitted use: a pull-quote attribution line, e.g. "— Jamie Hannah".)
- **Rule-of-three triads** — avoid "X, Y and Z" patterns piled up ("safe, efficient and under warranty"). Rewrite to one or two concrete points.
- **Corporate-speak:** leverage, utilise (use "use"), synergy, one-stop shop, solutions, seamless, bespoke (overused), cutting-edge, passionate about, committed to excellence.
- **Hollow reassurance stacked up:** "honest, thorough, reliable, professional, friendly" all at once — pick one true, specific thing.

## Vocabulary glossary (fixed spellings)
- **Gas Safe** (two words, capitalised) · **Gas Safe registered** · registration **977838**.
- **CP12** (no space) = Landlord Gas Safety Certificate.
- **Burgess Hill**, **Mid Sussex**, **West Sussex**, postcode area **RH15**. Towns: Haywards Heath, Hassocks, Cuckfield, Ditchling, Lindfield, Wivelsfield, Keymer.
- **WhatsApp** (capital W, capital A). **Google**.
- **boiler servicing / boiler repairs / gas safety certificate / gas hob installs** (the four services, lowercase in prose).
- Brand mark: **JDH Gas Services** (full) / **JDH Gas** (short).

## Punctuation
- **No em dashes** (above). Hyphens for compounds; commas/full stops for breaks.
- **Serial (Oxford) comma: yes** ("servicing, repairs, and gas safety") — *be consistent*; the homepage mostly uses "X, Y and Z" without it, so either is acceptable but don't mix within a sentence.
- **Smart quotes** in rendered copy (`&ldquo;…&rdquo;`, `&rsquo;`/`&apos;`).
- **Exclamation marks: rationed** — at most one per page, only in genuine warmth (success confirmations excepted: avoid them there too).
- **Case:** headlines & H1/H2 = sentence case ("Your local gas engineer in Burgess Hill"). Buttons & nav = sentence case ("WhatsApp me", "Call …"). Eyebrows = UPPERCASE (handled by `.eyebrow`).

## Numbers
- Spell out **one–nine**; numerals **10+**. Exception: prices and the £75 offer are always numerals.
- **Currency:** `£75` (no decimals unless pence). **Phone:** display `07544 063330`; `tel:+447544063330`.
- Times/days: "Monday to Friday".

## Tone by context
- **Marketing (hero/services/CTA):** warm, plain, confident. Lead with what you get, not adjectives.
- **Supporting microcopy:** helpful, brief.
- **Error messages:** state what happened + what to do; never blame the user ("Please add your postcode." not "Invalid input").
- **Success confirmations:** specific, calm — "Thanks — I've got your message. I'll be in touch shortly." (the one place the attribution-style dash is fine). No exclamation pile-ons.
- **Empty states:** offer the next action, not an apology.
- **Form labels/hints:** short, sentence case; hints in muted text; mark optional fields "(optional)".
- **Legal:** plainer than a lawyer's draft; short sentences; define terms.
