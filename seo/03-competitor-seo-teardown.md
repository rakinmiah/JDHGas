## Synthesis — gaps JDH can exploit

**Content gaps (no local competitor does these well):**
- **Answer-first CP12 page** with transparent price + legal-requirement list + FAQ schema → wins AI Overview (Smart Heat/Lindfield only partially do this).
- **Boiler fault/troubleshooting FAQ** (losing pressure, fault codes, not firing) absorbed into repair page → captures consideration-stage searchers; competitors leave this to manufacturers.
- **"What's included in a service"** with the real photo set + flue-gas-analysis explainer → none show the actual process with real engineer photos.

**Structural gaps:**
- Most local rivals are **faceless** (no named engineer/photo). JDH has Jamie + a strong portrait + real job photos → a decisive E-E-A-T/trust edge for a YMYL trade.
- Few show **transparent pricing** (only LGS £70+VAT, G&K £83/hr). Transparent pricing is a conversion + differentiation gap.
- **Reviews not surfaced on-site** by B.Cooke/Smart Heat despite high Google counts → JDH surfacing its 5.0(24) on-site with schema is an easy win.

**Query gaps (weak SERPs JDH can win):**
- `boiler repair [town]` local pack (JDH currently absent — fix GBP services).
- `gas safety certificate [smaller town]` (Cuckfield, Ditchling, Wivelsfield) — low competition.
- `gas hob/cooker installation burgess hill` — almost no dedicated local pages.

**SERP-feature gaps (winnable):**
- **AI Overview citation** on CP12 queries — currently Checkatrade/LGS/Smart Heat; JDH absent.
- **Image pack** — JDH's real job photos can win image results competitors lack.
- **PAA answers** — no local competitor owns the PAA boxes; answer-first FAQ blocks can.

**Topical-authority gaps:**
- No local competitor deeply owns the **landlord/letting-agent** angle as a system (Smart Heat is closest). A landlord-focused CP12 hub + agent-friendly booking is open territory.

**Backlink gaps:** UNMEASURED (no tools).
