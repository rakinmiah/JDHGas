## Strategic frame
JDH's winnable territory = **local commercial** (`[service] [town]`, `near me`) where the **Google Business Profile + local site** win, plus the **landlord/CP12 content** niche (AI-Overview + organic). National informational heads are conceded to manufacturers/forums and only reused as on-page FAQ. Few high-quality pages over volume (solo capacity).

## Negative-intent list (do NOT target)
- `cheap / cheapest / free [service] burgess hill` — JDH competes on quality/trust, not price (PASF shows these exist; cede them). Honest pricing yes; "cheapest" positioning no.
- `emergency / 24 hour / out-of-hours gas engineer` — unless Jamie confirms out-of-hours availability (open Q). Currently closes 5pm; the emergency pack is 24/7-only.
- `boiler installation / new boiler [cost]` as a *primary* target — Jamie's IG shows installs, but the SERP for "new boiler" is dominated by BOXT/Heatable/finance players and installs aren't his core advertised service. Mention as a capability; don't build a pillar to compete with national installers. (Revisit if Jamie wants install leads.)
- `commercial gas engineer` heavy targeting — primary audience is domestic + landlords; light mention only.
- `plumber / plumbing burgess hill` — JDH is gas/heating, not general plumbing. Don't dilute into plumbing SERPs.
