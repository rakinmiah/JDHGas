## Answer-first rule (for every question/FAQ)
Lead with the direct one-sentence answer, then 30–60 words elaboration, then deeper detail. This wins People-Also-Ask, featured snippets, and AI Overview citation. The **CP12 page especially** must open with the extractable legal answer — that's how we displace Checkatrade/Smart Heat Experts in the AI Overview Google currently shows.

## Editorial standards
- One H1/page; H2s structure the argument; don't skip levels.
- Titles `<keyword + town> | JDH Gas Services` ≤60 chars; meta descriptions ≤160, ending in a CTA (Call/WhatsApp/Book).
- Image alt text: describe the photo honestly first; absorb a keyword only if accurate. Use the real job photos, not stock.
- Cite authoritative sources for legal/safety claims (Gas Safe Register, GOV.UK gas safety regs) — links build YMYL trust.
- Dates: show "last updated" on the CP12 page (regs can change); footer year auto-derives from build.
- NAP (name / Burgess Hill–RH15 / 07544 063330) identical everywhere, matching the Google Business Profile.

## Freshness & kill policy
- Review service + town pages annually (prices, hours, reviews, regs current). Refresh CP12 page whenever gas-safety regulation changes.
- Kill/redirect any page that never gains traction rather than keeping thin content.

## Reviews are content too
After each job, Jamie requests a Google review (one-tap link). Target 2–4/month. Ask landlords to mention "CP12 / gas safety certificate". Surface reviews on-site with schema. This is the highest-ROI "content" activity.

## Anti-patterns (auto-reject)
- "Welcome to JDH Gas Services, your trusted gas engineer in Burgess Hill…"
- Footer keyword dump of towns.
- The same FAQ block pasted on every page.
- Exact-match question H2 stacks with no answers.
- Alt text that's just a keyword.
- Competing with British Gas / Worcester Bosch on generic "how often should a boiler be serviced" as a standalone post — that SERP is theirs; keep those Q&As inside service-page FAQ blocks with a local angle instead.
- "Cheapest", "emergency/24-hour" (unconfirmed), general plumbing framing.
