# Phase 10 — Authority & Content Strategy

_For a solo local trade, "authority" = local trust signals (GBP, reviews, citations) + light evergreen content + a credible named expert. NOT a content-marketing treadmill. Capacity is very low — the plan reflects that._

## Priority order (highest ROI first)
1. **Google Business Profile** (see Phase 8) — claim, optimise, add services/areas, photos, reviews. This is 80% of local authority for a trade.
2. **Review generation engine** — the single biggest compounding asset.
3. **Local citations** (NAP consistency).
4. **Few evergreen pages** (the service + town pages already planned).
5. **Light digital PR / community** — slow burn, optional.

## Review generation engine (do this relentlessly)
JDH is at 5.0×24 — already the rating leader locally, but volume can grow (rivals: B.Cooke 299, Smart Heat 366). More reviews → stronger local pack + more branded trust + AI/Reddit citation.
- **Process:** after every job, Jamie sends a one-tap Google review link (short link / QR card / WhatsApp message). Target **2–4 new reviews/month**.
- Reply to every review (signals activity to Google).
- Surface them on `/reviews` with Review schema (Phase 7) and seed a couple on the homepage.
- Ask happy landlords specifically (repeat, high-value) for reviews mentioning "CP12 / gas safety certificate" — keyword-rich reviews help.

## Author / E-E-A-T strategy (YMYL)
- **Single named author: Jamie Hannah** — Gas Safe registered (977838). `/about` is his author/credential page; `Person` schema links from the business + service pages (Phase 7).
- Surface credentials everywhere: Gas Safe number, real photo, real job photos, real reviews. For a YMYL trade this *is* the E-E-A-T — no separate medical/financial reviewer needed.
- **External credibility (light):** ensure JDH appears on the **Gas Safe Register "find a business"** listing (authoritative .gov-adjacent citation), and **Worcester Bosch / Vaillant "find an installer"** if Jamie holds accreditations (open Q — confirm). Each is a credible inbound link/citation.
- If AI drafts any blog/FAQ copy, Jamie reviews + owns it (no author-less content).

## Local citations (NAP consistency — set once, keep identical)
Build/claim consistent listings: **Checkatrade** (also a referral source + ranks organically), **Yell**, **Trustatrader / MyBuilder** (optional), **Bing Places**, **Apple Business Connect** (Apple Maps), **Facebook** business page, **Nextdoor** business. Same name/phone/area everywhere. These are the "backlink/citation" strategy for a trade (formal backlinks are secondary).

## Content plan (low cadence, evergreen-first)
Capacity is one busy engineer + a part-time site builder — so **no blog treadmill**. Ship the planned pages well, then maintain:
- **Build order:** home → 4 service pages → Burgess Hill + Hassocks town pages (JDH already ranks → reinforce) → Haywards Heath + Cuckfield (gap/low-comp) → reviews/gallery/about. Phase-2 villages only if time.
- **Evergreen FAQ depth** on service pages (answer-first) is the content investment — it serves PAA/AI citation without new pages.
- **Optional, only if appetite:** 2–4 genuinely useful seasonal posts/year, NOT competing with manufacturers — e.g. "When to book your boiler service before winter (Mid Sussex)", "Landlord gas safety: what Burgess Hill landlords need to know". Local angle = winnable; generic "how often to service a boiler" = not (Phase 2).
- **GBP posts:** short seasonal posts (pre-winter service reminder Sep–Oct; cold-snap breakdown availability) — cheap, boosts GBP.

## Original-data asset (cheap, pitchable, future)
Jamie accumulates real data: number of boilers serviced, most common faults, brands seen. Once there's volume, a simple "What I found servicing [N] boilers in Mid Sussex this year" post is genuinely original and locally pitchable (parish newsletters, local FB groups, Mid Sussex Times). Low priority but free differentiation.

## Community / social (slow burn, optional)
- **Instagram** (@jdhgasservices) — already active with strong job content; keep posting, link to site. Feeds image/brand search + AI citation over time.
- **Local Facebook groups / Nextdoor** (Burgess Hill, Haywards Heath, Hassocks community groups) — genuine helpfulness + recommendations; these drive real local leads for trades and are increasingly cited by AI. Contribute, don't spam.
- Reddit/Mumsnet: low priority (national, time-cost) — note that forums rank on informational queries, but it's not worth Jamie's time vs reviews + GBP.

## Backlink posture
No link-building campaign. Earn citations via the listings above + Gas Safe Register + manufacturer installer directories + local community mentions. Set up GSC for free backlink visibility. Unlinked-mention recovery becomes relevant only once brand search grows.

## Content refresh / kill
- Review service + town pages annually (prices, hours, reviews current; footer year auto). Refresh CP12 page if gas-safety regs change.
- Kill/redirect any thin page that never gains traction rather than hoarding it.
