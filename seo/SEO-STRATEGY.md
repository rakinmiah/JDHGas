_Compiled 2026-05-25. Organic-only. Free tools. Full phase files in `seo/`. `[STAND-IN — pending /plan-brand]` for voice._

## Executive summary

**Project:** SEO strategy for the JDH Gas Services website rebuild (Next.js, same domain jdhgas.co.uk). Sole trader **Jamie Hannah**, Gas Safe registered (977838) gas/heating engineer in **Burgess Hill, West Sussex**. Services: boiler servicing, boiler repairs, landlord gas safety certificates (CP12), gas appliance fitting.

**Goal:** lead generation (calls / WhatsApp / form enquiries). **Geo:** Burgess Hill + Haywards Heath, Hassocks, Cuckfield + Mid Sussex villages. **Timeline:** months. **Paid:** out of scope (organic-only). **YMYL:** yes (gas safety) — satisfied by Gas Safe reg + named engineer + real reviews/photos.

**North-star:** **40 qualified organic+GBP enquiries/month within 12 months** (re-baseline after 30 days of real data — none exists yet).

### The decisive finding
**JDH already owns the Burgess Hill local pack** — #1 with **5.0 from 24 Google reviews**, an existing Google Business Profile, and almost no website behind it. The entire strategy leverages this: optimise the GBP, build a fast schema-rich local site to convert the traffic it already attracts, and close the few gaps where JDH is absent.

### Top 5 quick wins
1. **Claim/verify + optimise the Google Business Profile** — add Boiler Repair, CP12, Gas Appliances as services; add Haywards Heath/Cuckfield to service area; point website link to the new site. (Fixes the repair-pack + Haywards-Heath absences.)
2. **Surface the 5.0 / 24 reviews on-site** with Review/AggregateRating schema (competitors don't).
3. **Answer-first CP12 page** to win the AI Overview currently citing Checkatrade & Smart Heat Experts.
4. **Name & show Jamie** (photo, story, Gas Safe number) — rivals are faceless; decisive E-E-A-T edge for a YMYL trade.
5. **Review-generation habit** — 2–4 new Google reviews/month via a one-tap link.

### Top 5 long plays
1. Town pages that rank beyond Burgess Hill (Haywards Heath, Cuckfield, villages).
2. Transparent pricing as a differentiator (most rivals hide it).
3. Landlord/letting-agent positioning (digital CP12, reminders, agent liaison — match Smart Heat Experts).
4. Image-pack presence from real job photos.
5. Local community + IG presence feeding brand search + AI citation.

**Paid posture:** none now; ready-to-activate Search stub in `09-paid-strategy.md` for later (esp. Haywards Heath or seasonal surges).

**Measurement stack:** GSC, GA4, GBP Insights, manual SERP checks, one spreadsheet. All free.

### Top 3 risks
1. **YMYL accuracy** — every gas-safety/legal claim must be correct (CP12 facts verified against the 1998 regs).
2. **Name collision** with "JD Heating and Plumbing" (Burgess Hill) — own the "jdh gas" branded SERP to avoid leaking clicks.
3. **No baseline + no paid tools** — volumes are SERP-read estimates; the north-star number must be re-set once GSC/GA4/GBP data exists.
